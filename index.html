About Us
A Second Home for Your Little One

Dev Little Stars is a warm, nurturing playschool and daycare in Kulathupalayam, Thondamuthur, Coimbatore — dedicated to creating a joyful and safe space where young children learn, explore, and grow.

Our thoughtfully designed curriculum blends three proven early learning approaches — Froebel, Montessori, and Play-Based Learning — ensuring every child experiences holistic development through creativity, hands-on activities, and meaningful play.

With small group sizes, personalized attention, and passionate educators, every child receives the love and guidance they truly deserve. We treat your child as our own.

"Every child is a star — we just help them shine brighter every single day." ✨

Our Approach
3 Proven Learning Methods

We blend the world's best early childhood education philosophies to give your child the strongest possible start in life.

01
🌱

Froebel Approach

Based on Friedrich Froebel's philosophy that children learn best through play, creativity, and connection with nature.

  • Hands-on exploration with natural materials
  • Songs, movement & outdoor play
  • Nurtures imagination & creativity
  • Child-led discovery activities
02
🔵

Montessori Method

Dr. Maria Montessori's approach focuses on independence, freedom within structure, and respect for the child's natural development.

  • Self-paced, independent learning
  • Specially designed learning materials
  • Mixed-age group interaction
  • Builds concentration & discipline
03
🎮

Play-Based Learning

Research-backed approach where children develop cognitive, social and emotional skills through purposeful, joyful play.

  • Role-play & dramatic activities
  • Collaborative group games
  • Story-telling & language development
  • Problem-solving through play
